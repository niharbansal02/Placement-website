Summer Internships

Salient Features

  • Internship recruitment model is aimed to incorporate the recent trend of companies to hire via the intern route as opposed to campus placements.
  • The budding talent among the Pre-Final year students can be identified and given PPOs.
  • Interns, having worked in the company and groomed by their culture may prefer to continue in the organization as a full-time hire after graduation.
  • Once the interns are selected, there can be a year-long orientation wherein they can be guided on what all they must become adept in and what exactly would they be expected to do at the organization, thereby ensuring that the student is productive from Day 1.
